Maggi

Ingredients

  • Maggi
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Vegetables
    • Capsicum
    • Carrot
    • Peas
    • Ginger
    • Garlic
    • Tomato
    • Coriander leaves

How to Make

  1. First, take Maggi and put it into boiling water. Once cooked, drain all the water and keep the Maggi dry.
  2. Cut all the vegetables into small pieces.
  3. Now peel the ginger and garlic.
  4. Take the tomato, roast it over the flame, and peel off the skin.
  5. Put the roasted tomato, ginger, garlic, and coriander leaves into a mixer jar and grind them into a paste.
  6. Take a pan, add some oil, and first fry the vegetables.
  7. Remove the vegetables from the pan.
  8. Now pour the paste into the pan, add some water, and cook for 2â€“3 minutes.
  9. After that, transfer it to a plate, add the Maggi and vegetables, and mix them all together.
